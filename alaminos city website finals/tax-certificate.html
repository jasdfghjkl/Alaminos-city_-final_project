Issuance of Community Tax Certificate

Office Division: One Stop Business Center (OSBC)
Classification: Simple
Type of Transaction: Government to Citizen, Government to Business, Government to Government
Who may Avail? All citizens who need a Community Tax Certificate (Cedula) for various purposes..

Requirement Location Action Fee Time
Community Tax Certificate Declaration Form One Stop Business Center Fills out Community Tax Declaration Form and proceeds to CTC Counter. None 5 minutes
Valid Identification Card (ID) One Stop Business Center Accepts the form and required documents. None 5 minutes
Proof of income, pay slip, BIR Form 2316 One Stop Business Center Pays the required amount. The amount depends on the income of the taxpayer. Please refer to the Table of Taxes and Fees. 3 minutes
Authorization Letter/SPA (if representative) One Stop Business Center Receives payment and issues the Community Tax Certificate. None 3 minutes
Total Processing Time 8 minutes

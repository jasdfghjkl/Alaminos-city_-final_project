Applying for a New Business Permit

Office Division: One Stop Business Center (OSBC)
Classification: Simple
Type of Transaction: Government to Citizen
Who may Avail? All citizens who plan to operate a tricycle business in the City.

Requirement Department Action Fee Time
Duly Accomplished Application Form One Stop Business Center 1.1 Submits application form together with requirements None 5 minutes
  • DTI Registration
  • SEC Registration
  • CDA Registration if change of business name/corporate name
  • Department of Trade and Industry (DTI)
  • Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC)
  • Cooperative Development Authority (CDA)
 1.1 Accepts and verifies completeness of application. Endorses application to appropriate regulatory section. None 5 minutes
Certificate of Lease/Proof of ownership if transfer of location One Stop Business Center 1.2 Application for transfer of location and additional line of business is inspected by the Composite Team. None 10 minutes
Letter of transfer of business ownership (for transfer of ownership) One Stop Business Center 1.3 Issues necessary clearances/certifications. None 5 minutes
Fire Safety Inspection Certificate (for transfer of location) Bureau of Fire Protection 1.4 Assesses additional regulatory fees and prepares order of payment. Releases order of payment. None 5 minutes
Authorization Letter/SPA (if representative) One Stop Business Center 2.1 Pays the necessary fees Please Refer to Table of Taxes, Fees and Charges 3 minutes
One Stop Business Center 2.1 Accepts payment and issues Official Receipt Please Refer to Table of Taxes, Fees and Charges 3 minutes
One Stop Business Center 3.1 Claims permit None 3 minutes
One Stop Business Center 3.1 Incorporates amendment and prints the permit. None 3 minutes
One Stop Business Center 3.2 Reviews permit. None
One Stop Business Center 3.3 Releases the business permit. None
Total Processing Time 31 minutes

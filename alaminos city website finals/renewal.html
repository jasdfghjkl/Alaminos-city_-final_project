Applying for a New Business Permit

Office Division: One Stop Business Center (OSBC)
Classification: Simple
Type of Transaction: Government to Business
Who may Avail? Business owners within the territorial jurisdiction of the City.

Requirement Department Action Fee Time
Duly Accomplished Business Application Form One Stop Business Center, Official Website of Alaminos City 1.1 Submits duly accomplished business application form together with documentary requirements. None 3 minutes
Community Tax Certificate (Original and Photocopy) One Stop Business Center 1.1 Receives the application form and checks completeness of the attached documentary requirements. None 3 minutes
Barangay Business Clearance (Original and Photocopy) Barangay Hall 1.2 BINning personnel validates information. None 2 minutes
Health Certificate (For Food Handlers and Health Care Services) City Health Office 1.3 CEO verifies compliance to the National Building Code. None 2 minutes
Zoning Clearance One Stop Business Center 1.4 CHO verifies compliance with Sanitation Code. None 2 minutes
One Stop Business Center 1.5 CTO-RPT attaches RPT Billing Statement. None 2 minutes
One Stop Business Center 1.6 CPDO verifies compliance with Zoning Regulations. None 2 minutes
One Stop Business Center 1.7 Assesses and consolidates fees to be paid in one billing statement. None 5 minutes
One Stop Business Center 1.8 Reviews and approves assessed taxes and fees. None 2 minutes
One Stop Business Center 1.9 Receives billing statement. Releases approved billing statement. None 1 minute
Authorization Letter/SPA (if representative) One Stop Business Center 2.1 Presents the billing statement to the cashier. Please Refer to Table of Taxes, Fees and Charges 2 minutes
One Stop Business Center 2.1 Accepts payment and issues Official Receipt. Please Refer to Table of Taxes, Fees and Charges 2 minutes
One Stop Business Center 3.1 Resubmits business application form after paying. Receives application form and verifies completeness of attached requirements. None 1 minute
One Stop Business Center 3.2 Prints business permit. None 1 minute
One Stop Business Center 3.3 Reviews permit. None 1 minute
One Stop Business Center 3.2 Receives business permit. Releases business permit and license. None 1 minute
Total Processing Time 30 minutes

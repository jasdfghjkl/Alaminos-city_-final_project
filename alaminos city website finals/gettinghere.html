Getting here

From Manila, the capital of the Philippines, Alaminos City lies approximately 205 kilometers away. The journey typically takes around 4 to 5 hours by any land transportation, making it accessible for travelers seeking a scenic escape. Alternatively, if you find yourself in Baguio, the picturesque summer capital of the Philippines, the distance to Alaminos City is approximately 124 kilometers, which translates to a pleasant 2-hour drive by any land transportation. Whether you're navigating from Manila's urban sprawl or soaking in Baguio's cool mountain air, Alaminos City awaits with its vibrant culture, stunning landscapes, and the famous Hundred Islands National Park, promising an unforgettable adventure for every traveler.

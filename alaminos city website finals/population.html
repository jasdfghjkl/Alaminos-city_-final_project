Population

The city has a land area of 164.26 square kilometers or 63.42 square miles which constitutes 3.01% of Pangasinan's total area. Its population as determined by the 2020 Census was 99,397. This represented 3.14% of the total population of Pangasinan province, or 1.88% of the overall population of the Ilocos Region. Based on these figures, the population density is computed at 605 inhabitants per square kilometer or 1,567 inhabitants per square mile.

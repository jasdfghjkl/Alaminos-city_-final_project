Mayor. Art Bryan Celeste

Bryan Celeste is a Filipino politician who is currently serving as the mayor of Alaminos City in Pangasinan Province, Ilocos Region, Philippines. He was elected as mayor in 2019 at the age of 22, making him the youngest ever to be elected in that position in the whole province of Pangasinan.

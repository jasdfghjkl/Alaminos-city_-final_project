Applying for a New Business Permit

Office Division: One Stop Business Center (OSBC)
Classification: Simple
Type of Transaction: Government to Citizen, Government to Business, Government to Government
Who may Avail? Clients requesting for certification of business record.

Requirement Agency Action Fees to be paid Time
Valid ID of owner Client proceeds to Releasing of Permit Section and presents valid IDs and other requirements. None 5 minutes
Authorization Letter/SPA (if representative) Verifies if business permit was issued. Issues order of payment when permit is verified. None 5 minutes
Pays the necessary fee at the Miscellaneous Counter. Accepts payment and issues Official Receipt. Php 100.00 3 minutes
Submits OR to the Releasing Section. Signs the photocopy/machine copy of business permit. Releases the Certified Copy of business permit. None 2 minutes
Total Processing Time 12 minutes
